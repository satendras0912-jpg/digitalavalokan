About Digital Avalokan

Digital Avalokan is focused on building AI-powered systems that transform how real estate businesses capture, qualify and convert leads.

Why Digital Avalokan Exists

Real estate businesses generate thousands of property inquiries every month through advertising, property portals and social media. However, a large percentage of these leads never convert into real opportunities because follow-ups are slow, manual and inconsistent.

Most sales teams rely heavily on spreadsheets, delayed calls and unstructured processes. As a result, valuable leads are lost and conversion rates remain low. Digital Avalokan was created to solve this problem by building AI-driven systems that manage the entire sales pipeline intelligently.

What We Build

AI Lead Qualification

AI voice agents automatically call and qualify property inquiries, helping sales teams focus only on serious prospects.

Automated Follow-ups

Smart WhatsApp automation ensures every lead receives timely communication without manual effort.

Sales Intelligence

Data-driven dashboards provide insights into lead quality, conversion performance and pipeline health.

Sales Automation

End-to-end automation connects ads, lead capture, qualification and follow-up into a single system.

Our Long-Term Vision

Digital Avalokan aims to become the intelligence layer for modern real estate businesses. Our goal is to build a complete ecosystem of AI-driven tools that help brokerages and developers automate their entire sales pipeline.

The first step in this journey is our flagship product SalesSetu, an AI-powered lead qualification and follow-up automation system designed specifically for real estate sales teams.
