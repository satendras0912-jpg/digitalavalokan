About Digital Avalokan

Digital Avalokan is building the intelligence layer for modern real estate sales using AI-powered automation systems.

Our Story

Real estate businesses generate thousands of property inquiries every month, but a large portion of these leads never convert into real opportunities. The problem is not demand — it is inefficient sales systems.

Most brokerage teams rely on manual follow-ups, spreadsheets and delayed responses, which results in lost leads and reduced conversions. Digital Avalokan was created to solve this problem by building intelligent automation systems that manage the entire lead lifecycle.

What We Build

AI Lead Qualification

Automated systems that instantly qualify property inquiries using AI voice agents and smart data analysis.

Automated Follow-ups

WhatsApp automation ensures every lead receives timely follow-ups without manual intervention.

Sales Intelligence

Data-driven dashboards help brokerage teams understand pipeline performance and conversion trends.

Our Vision for the Future

Digital Avalokan aims to become the intelligence infrastructure for modern real estate businesses. Our goal is to build a complete ecosystem of AI-driven tools that help sales teams capture, qualify and convert leads with minimal manual effort.

The first step in this journey is our flagship product, SalesSetu, which focuses on AI-powered lead qualification and follow-up automation.
